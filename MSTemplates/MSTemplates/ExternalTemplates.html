External templates

These emails were made to be sent to customers we have not sold to yet. This includes quotes and also emails that contain basic information. They contain an Unsubscribe link and will replace Constant Contact.

These need a print version that can be sent out with merge fields to a campaign in small batches.

Quote Templates

Template Description
Basic Quote This is the starting point for any quote--it can be adjusted as needed.
Yearly Renewal Quote This is a quote that you would send to a customer at the beginning of a new season.

Information Templates

Template Description
Traditional Inquiry This email is sent to customers who have sent in a squad alert or who we have had a brief conversation with, but whom we have not sold to yet.
All-Natural Inquiry This email is sent to customers who are interested in the all-natural treatment, but whom we have not sold to yet.
Request For Reviews An email sent to customers asking them to review our company.
Season Packages This email is sent to customers who have requested season package information
