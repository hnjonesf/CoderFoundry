Internal Templates

These emails are sent to customers to give them their invoices, alert them to cancellations, and give them more info about our service. I have not included images on the invoice templates. Is this a good idea, or do images tap the emotion (and value) of our services.

Invoice Templates

Template Description
Credit Card Expiring An automated email sent to customers whose credit cards are about to expire.
Default Invoice The basic invoice we send out to customers who purchase our service.
Invoice Reminder An automated email we send to customers who have not paid their invoice.
Payment Reminder An automated reminder to send out to customers set up on payment plans a couple days before their card will be charged.
Payment Rejected An automated email sent to customers whose payment has been declined.
Recurring Payment An email sent out to confirm a recurring payment.
Default Refund This email is sent out to customers as a reciept for their refund.
Default Credit Memo This email is sent out to customers as a reciept for their credit.

Service Templates

Template Description
Share The Squad An email sent out to customers asking them to promote us to their friends.
Default Service Dates This shows the customer all service dates left in the season.
Service Cancellation: Dogs This email is sent to customers who had dogs on their property at time of treatment.
Service Cancellation: Gates Locked This email is sent to customers who had gates locked on their property at time of treatment.
Service Cancellation: Holiday This email is sent to customers who had treatments scheduled on a holiday.
Service Cancellation: Rain This email is sent to customers when it rained on the day of treatment.
Service Cancellation: Workers This email is sent to customers who had workers on their property at time of treatment.
Service Date Reminder This email is sent to customers the day before their treatment.
Initial Service Follow Up This is sent to customers a few days after their first treatment to make sure they are satisfied.
Season Packages Follow Up This email is sent after the initial trial spray is done.
Service Date Complete This email is sent from the mobile app in the field as the treatment is completed.
